What is Hacathon ?

A hackathon is a focused 24-hour event where students, designer, entrepreneurs,
programmers, and problem solver come together in small teams to share ideas and
build something new and exciting, alongside a whole bunch of like-minded people.
You learn immensely with the help of mentors, interact with companies and have fun!
Hackathons are very beginner friendly
